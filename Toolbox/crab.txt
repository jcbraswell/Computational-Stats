The original crab data set was analyzed by Agresti [1996] and 
came from a study by Brockmann [1996]. We downloaded the data set from
http://lib.stat.cmu.edu/datasets/agresti
and extracted two of the explanatory variables: 
carapace width (cm) and weight (kg). These are contained 
in the matrix X. The response variable is the number of 
male crabs (satellites) surrounding the nesting female/male couple. 
This is saved in the satellites vector.
